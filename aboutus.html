INTRO

Founded in 2007, PT. YASKAWA MAKMUR ABADI has been a trusted leader in the engineering and manufacturing of medium and low voltage electrical systems. With decades of experience, we specialize in designing and producing high-quality electrical switchboard panels tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our expertise extends to comprehensive mechanical engineering services, ensuring that every system we deliver is efficient and reliable. We are also at the forefront of Industry 4.0, incorporating cutting-edge IoT technologies to enhance the efficiency, connectivity, and intelligence of our systems. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has driven our success and positioned us as a key player in the industry.

Relentless Pursuit of Excellence: We are committed to continuous growth and improvement by delivering reliable, professional engineering services.
Empowering the Nation: Our growth fuels the ability to support and build an ever-increasing power infrastructure for the country.
Enduring Partnerships: We strive to forge long-term relationships with our clients, government, employees, and suppliers, united in our mission to strengthen the nation's power capabilities.

To become a leading professional engineering and supply company, recognized by industries and global development programs for our excellence and commitment.

To support our clients in developing, maintaining, and enhancing their industries to exceed expectations.
To help our clients increase profits by improving efficiency and maximizing availability.
To deliver the highest level of engineering expertise and quality products, ensuring complete client satisfaction.
